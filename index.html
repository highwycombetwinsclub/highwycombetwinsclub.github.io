The High Wycombe Twins Club is the twins and multiples club for High Wycombe and the surrounding villages. We meet fortnightly to provide a chance for parents (and parents ‘to be’) and carers of twins and multiples to meet up, chat, offer support and drink tea, while the children play.

Our Facebook site "lots of tots – High Wycombe Twins Club" has the most up to date information (including our calendar of events and meets)

We look forward to welcoming more twin and multiple families to the club!
